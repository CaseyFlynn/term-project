Analysis of Political Tweets

During the week before the primaries in Deleaware, Rhode Island, Maryland, and Pennsylvania, data about the top four presidential canidates: Hilary Clinton, Bernie Sanders, Donald Trump, and Ted Cruz, was collected from Twitter.

By analyzing the tweets where each canidate was mentioned against the primary election results, we were surprised by how close the percentage of votes for each canidate was to the percentage of tweets they were mentioned in.
